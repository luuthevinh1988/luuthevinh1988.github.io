Sapa - 2 nights on train / 2 nights at hotel

TOUR HIGHLIGHTS Sapa is a hill station which was built by the French in 1900, and locates at the high latitude of 1650m above the Sea level. Sapa is very famous for its beautiful mountainous landscape, the terrace rice fields, the hill tribes, the valley, the waterfalls.
This journey gives you a great train experience in Vietnam.
Highlights

  • Gain a better understanding of the lives of the ethnic minority communities in the North Vietnam
  • Sapa and its surroundings offer particularly good ground for trekking
  • visit the two Black H’mong & Red Dao villages and a waterfall.
  • Visit the villages inhabited by H’mong and Dzay people, respectively called Lao Chai and Ta Van

NIGHT 1: BOARDING THE TRAIN IN HANOI ( SOFT SLEEPER)
DAY 1: LAO CAI - SAPA - TREKKING
DAY 2: SAPA - WATERFALLS - CABLE CAR
DAY 3: SAPA - VILLAGE - LAO CAI - NIGHT TRAIN

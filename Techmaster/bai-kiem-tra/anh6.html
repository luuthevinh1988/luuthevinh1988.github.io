Dalat - Nha Trang - 5 Days 4 Nights

TOUR HIGHLIGHTS

Dalat and Nha Trang are very popular places in the South Vietnam, you can see the hill stations with many French influent buildings, and also the green cover in the mountain, amazing waterfalls. After Dalat, you will go down hill for the beaches. Nha Trang is the best beach in Vietnam with a long sandy beach.
